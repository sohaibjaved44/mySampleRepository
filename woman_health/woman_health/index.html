Our Aim

To connect students, especially those who utilize the internet for learning, to the concept of feedback based learning.

 

Why Online TechPlug?

The primary difference between learning using audio, video or written material over the internet and learning through a tutor is the concept of feedback. Students develop questions and doubts as they traverse through learning material and it is often seen the students do not hold onto these questions to ask later and they either assume answers or kill their curiosity. Supervised learning under the guidance of a tutor solves this problem very effectively as students can interrupt any learning session to get their curiosity addressed.

What we offer

A virtual classroom is not just as effective as a normal classroom but far better. The teacher can interact using the many helpful simulation and calculation software in addition to the normal voice and video conversation. The teacher can use either a video camera or simply share his/her screen which can be utilized as a whiteboard to write, draw and learn. At TechPlug, we take special care to select, train and share our experience with the hired tutors and to provide quality online tutoring experience for students.
